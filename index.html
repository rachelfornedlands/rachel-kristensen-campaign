About Me

Hello! I’m Rachel Kristensen — a passionate community member running for Local Council in Nedlands. I care deeply about responsible local planning, community wellbeing, sustainability, and transparent governance. I believe Nedlands can be a place where families, small businesses, and residents of all ages thrive.

My Priorities

  • Community-focused Planning — sensible planning that protects neighbourhood character while allowing thoughtful growth.
  • Environmental Sustainability — support initiatives that protect green spaces, biodiversity, and water quality.
  • Local Services & Safety — ensuring essential services, community centres and safe public spaces for all ages.
  • Transparency & Engagement — councillors who listen and keep residents informed.
